The model for all future lighthouses, the Pharos of Alexandira shone in the harbor of Alexandira in Egypt.

Standing over 350 feet tall, it had a mirror at the top to reflect sunlight during the day, a fire at night.

Unfortunately the lighthouse was damaged in several earthquakes and no longer stands.

Interested parties can visit the site where the lighthouse once stood and imagine the wonder it once held.
